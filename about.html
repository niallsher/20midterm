Our Mission

Since starting the company in 2001, our commitment has been to our customers. We are focused on gathering the best materials to make the best products and bringing them to our customers at the best prices.

Meet the team

Our team is here to help you. Our collective 100 years of experience will help us in providing you with the best of the best products and services.


Niall Sheridan comes from a long line of top-quality jewelers. His ancestry can be traced back to the man that first cut the Hope Diamond for Louis XIV. The knowledge of generations has been passed down to Niall, allowing him to craft the highest quality pieces on the market. Niall personally oversees the crafting of all of our luxury pieces for our clients.

Mallory Hood grew up travelling with her archaeologist parents. At age 10, she happened upon an untapped diamond deposit on a deserted island in the Pacific Islands. Since starting KMTN jewelers, these diamonds have been used exclusively for our luxury products. Mallory oversees the ethical and safe extraction of all of our top quality materials.

Tilly Rigby got her MBA from Harvard at the age of 18 as the youngest ever graduate. She has since used her unique talents to start 14 Fortune 500 companies since. Her business talents have helped KMTN Jewelers thrive while maintaining its commitment to customers and quality.

Kennedy May has developed her design expertise over the past 19 years at top international institutions throughout the world. Her eye for design and quality means that every item is maximized for comfort and aesthetic appearance. Kennedy's work has helped propel KMTN to the forefront of quality jewelry pieces, and has helped her win the International Award for Excellent Design for the last four years.

Our whole team is dedicated to making quality jewelry and making it accessible to the world.

We can't wait to help you achieve all of your jewelry dreams.
